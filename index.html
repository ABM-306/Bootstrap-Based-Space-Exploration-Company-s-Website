Groundbreaking

The Perseverance Rover : is exploring Mars' Jezero Crater, searching for ancient life and collecting rock samples. Equipped with cutting-edge instruments like MOXIE, which produces oxygen, and accompanied by the Ingenuity helicopter, it's revealing Mars' volcanic past and water history. Its discoveries, including organic molecules, are crucial for future missions and our understanding of the Red Planet.

(MRO) is Remote sensing satellite : discovered signs of ancient underground lakes on Mars Using its imaging spectrometer, MRO detected hydrated minerals in dry gullies, suggesting intermittent liquid water flows This finding hints at Mars' potential to support life and provides valuable insights into its climate history.
